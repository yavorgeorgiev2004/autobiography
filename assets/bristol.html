Bristol

Restaurants and Food

Bristol’s diverse food scene is famous in the whole country. Za Za Bazaar a all you can eat resturant with loads of international food stations is known as one of the largest restaurants in the UK. Bristol also offers loads of botuique resturans one of which is Klosterhaus resturant.

Parks and Nature

Bristol has loads of parks and green spaces.Brandon Hill whith its tower view point which overlooks a lot of the city,Leigh Woods which has loads of forsets trails , Eastville Park, St George's Park and Sea Mills with riverside walks.

Festivals

Bristol hosts many festivals and events.One is Love Saves The Day which is a big music festival. Bristol Balloon Fiestal attracts many visitors for balloon flights and displays. St Paul’s Carnival celebrates Black culture with music and community stalls.

Activities and Entertainment

  • Go Karting: TeamSport and Absolutely Karting provide two level indoor karting.
  • Cinemas:two of the cinema options are Everyman and Vue.
  • Harbourside: Restaurants, nightlife and riverside events.
